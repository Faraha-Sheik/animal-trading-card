Horse

The horse is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus. The horse has evolved over the past 45-55 million years from a small multi-toed creature, Eohippus.

  • Scientific name: Equus caballus
  • Lifespan: 25 - 30 years
  • Popular Breed: The American Quarter Horse
  • Speed: 55 mph

Horses have been called the noblest of creatures. They have two sides to their brains: the left brain and the right brain. Horses have 205 bones in their skeleton. Male horse is called stallion, the female horse is called mare.
